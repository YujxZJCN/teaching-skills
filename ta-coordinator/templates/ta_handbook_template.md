## Calibration

Before the first consequential grading and before each new instrument, we run a norming
session (~{{session_length}}). Pre-work — independently scoring the anchor set — is
part of the job and is counted in your hours. Session decisions become rubric
annotations that bind all of us, including the professor. Disagreement in session is
welcome; it is how the rubric gets better.
