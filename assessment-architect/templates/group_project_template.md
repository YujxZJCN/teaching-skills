## Peer-Assessment Form *(confidential — seen by the professor, not your teammates)*

Rate **each teammate and yourself** on each factor, using the behavioral anchors. This is
about observable contribution — not who you like. {{Runs at: mid-project check-in + final.}}

| Factor | 5 — consistently | 3 — sometimes | 1 — rarely |
|--------|------------------|---------------|------------|
| Contributing to the team's work | delivered assigned work on time, full share | delivered most, some gaps | frequently missed deliverables |
| Interacting with teammates | communicated, responsive, constructive | mostly | often unresponsive |
| Keeping the team on track | helped plan, met deadlines | partially | did not engage |
| Expecting quality | pushed for good work, checked others' | accepted as-is | indifferent to quality |
| Relevant knowledge/skill, applied | applied skills to the team's benefit | partially | did not |

**For each teammate (and yourself):** name/initials, score per factor, and **one sentence
of evidence** for any score of 1 or 5. {{Self-rating is included so calibration is visible.}}
