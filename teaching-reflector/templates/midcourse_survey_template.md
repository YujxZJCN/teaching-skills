## Administration logistics (professor-facing — delete before distributing)

- **Timing:** week {{week_number}} — target window is week 4–6 of a 15–16 week term;
  earlier students can't judge, later there's no runway to adjust.
- **Channel:** {{anonymous form link | paper, collected by a student volunteer}}.
  Anonymity must be real and stated — no handwriting on named papers, no login-tracked
  forms unless responses are genuinely unlinked.
- **In class, 5 minutes, near the start** (end-of-class surveys get walked out on).
  In-class administration roughly doubles response rates vs "fill it out sometime."
- **Small class warning (N < ~10):** anonymity is thin and free text may be
  identifiable; consider a structured plus/delta discussion instead.
- **Close-the-loop date:** {{date — within one week of collection}}. Announcing this
  date when administering the survey raises both response rate and response quality.
